About Fluffy Puffy Paws INN:

At Fluffy Puffy Paws Inn, we are open 6 days a week. We are open at 9am- 5pm on Monday through Friday and 11am-7pm on Saturdays. Our store sells several Dog products like toys, leashes, food, and allow customers to adopt dogs too! We can be contacted through our email at Fluffypuffypawsinn@gmail.com and 1-234-567-8910. At the start of our grand opening, our store will have 2 groomers, 3 trainers, 5 care takers and 2 salespeople. Customers can book appointments to use our services of grooming, care taking, dog sitting and more.
